About SentrySearch

SentrySearch is built for people who need fast, explainable access to the exact video moment that matters. The product focuses on natural language search across dashcam footage, security footage, and incident review workflows where manual scrubbing is too slow and too inconsistent.

Why we built SentrySearch

We built SentrySearch because most video review workflows still depend on timestamps, memory, and patience instead of clear search. That gap is especially painful when a user needs to verify driving incidents, confirm a parking lot incident, or isolate a driveway camera event under time pressure. SentrySearch exists to make that workflow feel like a serious software product rather than a manual hunt.

Who SentrySearch is for

SentrySearch is for individual drivers, fleets, operators, property teams, and security users who need a professional way to search footage and export a matched clip. The product direction is intentionally practical: faster incident review, clearer evidence, and a workflow that can grow into a trusted SaaS experience.
