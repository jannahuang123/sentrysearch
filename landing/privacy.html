Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SentrySearch handles information when visitors access the website, request a demo, or contact the team about the product.

Information We Collect

SentrySearch may collect contact information you submit through forms or email, limited analytics data used to understand site performance, and technical logs needed to secure the website. The current landing page does not invite public video uploads.

How We Use Information

Information is used to respond to inquiries, improve the product and website, maintain security, and understand interest in product features such as local clip extraction, hosted workflows, and incident review.

Data Retention

SentrySearch keeps business records and support communications only as long as reasonably necessary for operations, legal compliance, and product support. Data that is no longer needed is deleted or de-identified according to internal retention practices.

Contact

For privacy questions, requests, or notices, contact privacy@sentrysearch.my.
